# Student Performance Report
## Summary
The student performance report is based on the analysis of the outputs from Task1, Task2, and Task3. A total of 10 students were analyzed, with 4 students passing all subjects with 80%+ attendance and 6 students failing to meet the criteria.

## Subject Performance
The subject performance is as follows:
- Tamil: 5 students passed, 5 students failed
- English: 6 students passed, 4 students failed
- Maths: 4 students passed, 6 students failed
- Science: 5 students passed, 5 students failed
- Social: 6 students passed, 4 students failed

## Attendance Analysis
The attendance analysis shows that 4 students had 80%+ attendance, while 6 students had less than 80% attendance.

## Actions Taken Summary
The following actions have been taken:
- Identified students who need extra support
- Categorized students based on their performance
- Analyzed subject-wise performance

## Recommendations
The following recommendations are made:
- Immediate actions for struggling students: Provide extra support and counseling to students who failed to meet the criteria.
- Subject-specific interventions: Focus on improving Maths and Science scores, as these subjects have the lowest pass rates.
- Attendance improvement strategies: Implement attendance tracking and monitoring systems to encourage students to attend classes regularly.
- Resource allocation suggestions: Allocate additional resources to support students who are struggling with specific subjects.
- Parent-teacher meeting recommendations: Schedule regular parent-teacher meetings to discuss student progress and provide feedback.
